Wait Till Season to Sell? Not So Fast.

Every June, two beliefs harden in South Florida. Buyers tell themselves they'll wait for summer, because summer is when the bargains show up. Sellers tell themselves they'll wait for season, because season is when the buyers show up. Only one survives contact with the data.

Across Palm Beach County, summer doesn't lower the price — it lowers the pace. The August 2025 median ran about 2% higher than the year before, even as transaction volume cooled. The deals signed in winter simply close in June and July.

This week's brief covers the seasonality data, why “Boca and Delray” isn't one market (central Boca at 2.7 months supply vs. coastal Palm Beach at nearly 16), how today's 6.53% rate quietly eats any seasonal discount, and the carrying-cost stack that does far more damage to a budget than the calendar ever could.
